# NAFC Universe Knowledge Base v1.0

## Overview

The NAFC Universe Knowledge Base is a centralized repository for domain-specific knowledge, providing context, data, and insights for the platform's AI models, agents, and decision-making processes. It is organized into several key domains to facilitate easy access and management.

## Knowledge Domains

-   **Architecture**: Architectural patterns, decision records, and reference architectures.
-   **AI**: Research, best practices, and model-specific knowledge.
-   **Agriculture**: Specialized knowledge for agricultural applications and models.
-   **Business**: Business rules, market models, and enterprise-specific knowledge.
-   **Manufacturing**: Industrial processes, factory models, and supply chain knowledge.
-   **Research**: Scientific papers, prototypes, and experimental findings.
-   **Security**: Security standards, threat intelligence, and vulnerability management.
-   **Marketing**: Customer models, marketing strategies, and campaign insights.
-   **Digital Twin**: Modeling techniques and data for digital twin applications.
-   **Knowledge Graph**: Schema definitions, relationship models, and graph data.
-   **World Model**: Data and models for representing complex real-world environments.
-   **Decision Engine**: Business rules, simulation data, and decision-making logic.

## Usage

This knowledge base is intended to be consumed by both humans and AI agents. It should be kept up-to-date with the latest findings and best practices in each domain.
